Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +

Our MOQ starts with 5 CBM (cubic meters). You can start with LCL (Less than Container Load) or scale to full container loads (FCL) based on your requirements. We're flexible and work with both small and large orders.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept secure international payment methods including bank wire transfers (TT), Letter of Credit (L/C) at sight, and PayPal for smaller orders. Payment terms such as FOB and EXW can be arranged as per buyer requirements.

How long does shipping take? +

Shipping times vary based on the destination country and shipping method. We offer air, sea, and road freight options. Sea shipments typically take 15-30 days depending on the destination port. We provide complete shipment tracking and documentation.

Can I request product samples before ordering? +

Yes, we provide product samples and packaging prototypes. This allows you to verify quality standards and market fit before committing to bulk orders. Sample costs and shipping are typically charged separately.

Do you offer private labeling services? +

Yes, we offer complete private label and white-label solutions. This includes custom recipes, packaging design, logo integration, and retail-ready products under your brand name.

What certifications do your products have? +

Our products come with all necessary export certifications including FSSAI, APEDA, Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Origin (COO), and country-specific compliance as per buyer requirements. We are ISO 9001:2015 certified.
